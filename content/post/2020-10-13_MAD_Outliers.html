In this post, I show how to set the cutoff for MAD-based outlier detection. This is not meant to be an explainer about MAD outlier detection since there’s already good resources (here)[] and (here)[].

A colleague recent came to me asking for advice about automated univarite outlier detection. I wasn’t all that familiar with outlier detection. After doing some reading, I found a method that suited our purposes well: outlier detection using the Median Absolute Deviation (MAD).
